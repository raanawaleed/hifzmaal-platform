# HifzMaal – Islamic Family Finance Management Platform

**HifzMaal** (حفظ المال) means *Protection of Wealth* — one of the core objectives of Maqasid al-Shariah.  
It is a modern, family-oriented personal finance web application designed to help Muslims manage, protect, and grow their wealth in a simple, secure, and Shariah-compliant way.

---

## Vision

To build the first complete Islamic family finance ecosystem for South Asia and beyond, combining:
- Practical money management
- Islamic financial principles
- Modern UX & technology

---

## Core Features

### Family & Household Finance
- Add family members (Spouse, Children, Dependents)
- Role-based access (Owner, Editor, Viewer, Approver)
- Shared household wallet
- Monthly family budget
- Spending limits & approval workflow
- Family-wise financial reports

### Income & Expense Management
- Category-based tracking
- Recurring transactions
- Receipt uploads
- Voice input (Urdu / English)
- Multi-currency support
- Smart categorization

### Utility & Bill Tracking
- Electricity, Gas, Water, Internet, Mobile, Rent, School Fees
- Due date reminders
- Auto recurring bills
- Bill splitting among family members
- Usage & cost history graphs
- Next bill estimation

### Zakat & Islamic Finance Module
- Automatic Nisab calculation (Gold & Silver based)
- Lunar (Hijri) year tracking
- Zakat on:
  - Cash & Savings
  - Gold & Silver
  - Business Inventory
  - Investments
  - Loans Receivable
- Family combined Zakat calculation
- Zakat due reminders
- Zakat & Sadaqah distribution tracking
- Recipient management

### Savings & Goals (Bachat)
- Hajj & Umrah
- Education
- Marriage
- Emergency Fund
- Business Capital
- Auto monthly savings
- Visual progress & dua reminders

### Smart Insights
- Overspending alerts
- Budget variance analysis
- Financial health score
- Barakah tips
- Halal spending classification

### Security & Privacy
- End-to-end encryption
- Two-factor authentication
- Islamic privacy principles (No Data Selling)
- Cloud & local backups

### Localization
- Languages: Urdu, English, Hindi, Bangla
- Regional currency support
- Islamic calendar integration
- Light & Dark mode
- Islamic-themed UI

---

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** Livewire, Vue.js  
- **Backend:** PHP, Laravel  
- **Database:** MySQL / PostgreSQL  
- **Auth:** Laravel Sanctum / Passport  
- **Charts & Visualizations:** Chart.js / ApexCharts  
- **Future Mobile App:** Laravel API + Vue Native / React Native  
- **Future AI:** Financial Insights Engine
